## What is NoirScroll?

**NoirScroll** is a high-performance, distraction-free manga and webtoon reader built around one philosophy: **the art comes first.**

No cluttered sidebars. No intrusive ads. No account required. Just you and the story.

It aggregates content from multiple providers for maximum availability, enriches metadata via AniList, and stores everything Рђћ your history, bookmarks, tags, and reading stats Рђћ **locally on your device**. Your data never leaves your hands.

---

## Features

### Core Reading Engine

The reader is the heart of NoirScroll, engineered to feel fast and fluid across every format.

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| **Webtoon Mode** | Continuous vertical scroll, optimized for Manhwa and Webtoon-style content |
| **Paged Mode** | Classic horizontal page-flip with full LTR and RTL direction support |
| **Pull-to-Transition** | Drag past the last page to smoothly transition into the next chapter |
| **Resume Reading** | Exact scroll position and page number saved per chapter, automatically |
| **Auto-Next Chapter** | Advances to the next chapter the instant you finish the current one |
| **Pinch-to-Zoom & Pan** | Full gesture support for zooming in on details, on mobile and desktop |
| **Chapter Progress Bar** | A sleek top-mounted bar shows your exact position within any chapter |
| **Tap-to-Toggle UI** | Tap the center of the screen to instantly show or hide all controls |

---

### Library & Organization

A centralized, fully offline hub for your reading collection.

- **Favorites** Рђћ Bookmark any title with a single tap to save it to your personal library.
- **Reading History** Рђћ Every opened title is automatically tracked alongside your last-read chapter.
- **Custom Tags / Folders** Рђћ Organize your library with tags you define yourself. Create labels like `Plan to Read`, `Dropped`, `Masterpieces`, or anything else. Filter your entire collection by tag instantly.
- **Read Status Tracking** Рђћ Chapters you've completed (90%+ read) are automatically greyed out in chapter lists so you always know exactly where you left off.

---

### Discovery & Search

- **Multi-Source Aggregation** Рђћ Fetches content from multiple providers (including Mangapill and AquaManga) simultaneously for broader coverage and higher availability.
- **Global Search** Рђћ One search query hits every supported source at once. Find any title regardless of which provider carries it.
- **Explore Page** Рђћ Browse trending and popular titles with high-resolution cover art and rich metadata.
- **AniList Integration** Рђћ Pulls detailed descriptions, genre tags, and community ratings from AniList for every title.

---

### Reading Statistics & Insights

A data-driven view of your reading habits, accessible from the Settings menu.

- ­ЪЊџ **Total Chapters Read** Рђћ A lifetime count of your reading activity.
- РЈ▒№ИЈ **Time Spent Reading** Рђћ Actual minutes tracked inside the reader.
- ­ЪјГ **Genre Breakdown** Рђћ A visual chart of your most-read genres (Action, Romance, Fantasy, and more).
- ­ЪЌЊ№ИЈ **Activity Log** Рђћ A record of your most recent reading sessions.

---

### Offline & PWA Capabilities

NoirScroll is a fully installable Progressive Web App.

- **Service Worker Caching** Рђћ The app shell and all assets are pre-cached via `vite-plugin-pwa`, keeping the app functional offline.
- **Smart Image Caching** Рђћ Recently read chapters and their images are cached automatically so you can continue reading without a connection.
- **Installable** Рђћ Add NoirScroll to your Home Screen on iOS or Android, or install it as a standalone desktop app. No app store required.
- **Bulk Download** *(Experimental)* Рђћ Manually prepare chapters for offline reading in advance.

---

### Personalization & Settings

- **Dark / Light Mode** Рђћ Fully responsive theming, defaulting to a deep Noir dark theme tuned for OLED screens.
- **Reading Direction** Рђћ Toggle between Left-to-Right (Western) and Right-to-Left (Manga) at any time.
- **Optimized Image Loading** Рђћ Fade-in animations and skeleton loaders prevent layout shifts and blank flashes during image fetch.
